Orchid Malevolence is an Item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Caster .

Additional information [ edit ]

  • One Orchid Malevolence grants a total of 30 attack speed , 275 mana , 1 (+ 150%) mana regeneration , 1.79% spell damage and 30 (55 to intelligence heroes) attack damage .

Ability [ edit ]

Soul Burn

Silences the target unit for 5 seconds and amplifies the damage it takes by 30%.

Effect persists and attempts to deal damage when expiring while spell immune if debuff was placed before spell immunity and when not dispelled.
Modifiers
Orchid Malevolence Debuff: Dispellable with any dispel.

Notes:

  • Does not directly amplify damage as it is dealt, but rather saves all damage values and deals 30% of it 5 seconds after cast.
    • This means that the damage can be avoided by becoming spell immune or invulnerable / hidden at the end of the duration.
  • The damage is applied as soon as the debuff expires. Dispelling the debuff, however, skips the damage entirely.
    • Recasting Soul Burn on the same target refreshes the duration.
    • Doing this delays the damage further, and gives more time to deal damage to the target.
    • The damage is credited to the last caster of Soul Burn on the target.
  • Only considers magical , physical and pure damage ( after all reductions ). Other sources of health loss are ignored.
  • Shares cooldown with Bloodthorn .


Recommended heroes [ edit ]

Core

Clinkz, Storm Spirit.

Situational

Silencer, Outworld Devourer, Queen of Pain, Nature's Prophet.

Recommend

Slark, Spirit Breaker, Invoker, Arc Warden.

Tips [ edit ]

  • Orchid is great against Intelligence heroes as they are dependent on their abilities to deal damage, but it is also effective against heroes with escape abilities like Weaver or Anti-Mage and strong initiation like Faceless Void and Enigma.
  • Get the item quickly before the enemy team gets items able to purge the silence.

Items Synergy [ edit ]

  • You can combine it with an item that provide mana burn like Diffusal Blade or Necronomicon to make your enemy unable to cast a spell after the silence is over.
