The Phase Boots are an item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Common . It can also be completed solely with items from the Side Lane Shop .

Additional information

  • Movement speed bonuses from multiple pairs of boots do not stack.
  • Stacks with all other movement speed bonuses.

Ability

Phase
Play
Ability
No Target
Affects
Self
Gives increased movement speed and lets you move through units. The increased movement speed is based on whether the equipped hero is melee or ranged.
Move Speed Bonus (Melee) : 24%
Move Speed Bonus (Ranged) : 20%
Duration : 2.5
8
Modifiers
Item Phase Boots Active: Undispellable.

Notes:

  • Does not interrupt the user's channeling spells upon cast.
  • Allows the user to pass through other units, including wards , but not through buildings .
  • Checks the user's range type periodically and updates the speed bonus immediately if the range type changes.
  • When used constantly, increases the user's average movement speed by 7.5% if melee, and by 6.25% if ranged.


Recommended heroes

  • Heroes who rely on damage multipliers and critical strike (innate or from items) will hit harder with Phase Boots.
    • Monkey King ​
    • Phantom Assassin ​
    • Brewmaster ​
    • Juggernaut ​
    • Sniper ​
    • Bloodseeker ​
    • Tusk ​
    • Bounty Hunter ​
  • Heroes who can hit multiple targets with their attacks will gain higher overall damage output with Phase Boots.
    • Ember Spirit ​
    • Gyrocopter ​
    • Medusa ​
    • Kunkka ​
  • Heroes with attack speed bonuses do not need the extra attack speed from Power Treads , and thus usually opt for Phase Boots instead.
    • Ursa ​
    • Troll Warlord ​
    • Windranger ​
    • Lifestealer ​
  • Heroes who simply need to a little extra speed and Phase for mobility purposes.
    • Night Stalker ​
    • Mirana ​
    • Clockwerk ​

Tips

  • Using Phase Boots will break invisibility , but not Smoke of Deceit .
  • Use Phase Boots to run through creeps in lane. This can help you catch up to an enemy, or flee from an attacker while they are blocked by the creeps' collision size .
  • Use Phase Boots when enemy heroes or summoned units attempt to body block you, especially at choke points.
