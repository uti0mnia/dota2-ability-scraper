The Heart of Tarrasque is an item listed at the Main Shop, under Armor . However, it is assembled entirely with items from the Secret Shop .

Additional information [ edit ]

  • One Heart of Tarrasque grants a total of 1300 health and 2.4 (+ 7% of max health) health regeneration (and 40 attack damage to strength heroes).

Ability [ edit ]

Health Regeneration
Ability
Passive
Affects
Self
Restores 7% of max health per second.

If damage is taken from an enemy hero or Roshan, this ability is disabled for 5 seconds for melee heroes, or 7 seconds for ranged heroes.
Max Health Restored per Second : 7%
Damage Cooldown (Melee) : 5
Damage Cooldown (Ranged) : 7
Gets disabled for illusions upon taking damage.
Illusions do not benefit from the health regen.

Notes:

  • Health Regeneration from multiple Hearts of Tarrasque does not stack.
  • Heals in the form of health regeneration, so it heals for 0.7% of maximum health in 0.1-second intervals.
  • Can fully heal the owner in 14.29 seconds.
  • Damage greater than 0 from any player (including allies, but not self) or Roshan puts the regeneration on a 5 / 7 -second cooldown.
    • Since it is a cooldown and not actually disabled, it can be refreshed with Refresher Orb and Rearm .
    • Is also affected by Cooldown Reduction from Octarine Core & Arcane Rune .
    • Does not get disabled by damage flagged as HP Removal .


Recommended heroes [ edit ]

  • Heroes whose default item build includes Heart of Tarrasque as a situational item:

Strength: Sven , Bristleback , Alchemist , Centaur Warrunner , Earthshaker , Tidehunter , Sand King , Clockwerk , Doom , Slardar , Tusk , Beastmaster , Lycan , Magnus , Axe , Chaos Knight , Io , Phoenix , Huskar , Brewmaster , Undying , Wraith King , Pudge , Night Stalker , Dragon Knight , Timbersaw , Abaddon .

Agility: Razor , Faceless Void , Bloodseeker , Spectre , Naga Siren , Ursa , Meepo , Terrorblade , Weaver , Phantom Lancer , Anti-Mage , Viper .

Intelligence: Dark Seer , Visage , Necrophos , Death Prophet .

Tips [ edit ]

  • Getting armor and evasion in combination increase your EHP a lot.
  • If you are low HP during a fight, escape and stay near the fight to regen then fight back.
  • A Tarrasque is effective against a team with a lot of burst magical and physical damage. The HP granted by the Tarrasque can tank two dagon level 5 (does 600 damage with 25 % magic resistance). You can also get a blademail to return the damage you tank.
  • It gives 40 damage to strength heroes, combine it with attack speed to increase your DPS.
  • As tempting as it may be, getting more than one heart of tarrasque is, in almost all situations, detrimental to your build, since the passive regen bonus doesn't stack and the cost of the item is very high, its generally advisable to purchuase other health boosting items such as Eye of Skadi and Octarine Core depending on the enemy team's composition

Trivia [ edit ]

  • The name "Tarrasque" references the Tarrasque , an almost undefeatable monster in Dungeons & Dragons , which in return references the Tarasque , a mythological dragon-hybrid that was tamed by Saint Martha.
