Eul's Scepter of Divinity is an item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Caster .

Additional information [ edit ]

  • Movement speed bonuses from multiple Eul's Scepters of Divinity fully stack.
  • Stacks with all other movement speed bonuses.
  • One Eul's Scepter of Divinity grants a total of 40 movement speed , 110 mana , 0.4 (+ 150%) mana regeneration and 0.71% spell damage (and 10 attack damage to intelligence heroes).

Notes:

  • Double clicking the item casts it on self.
  • Applies a basic dispel on the target upon cast.
  • Can only target enemies, self or own Meepo clones and Tempest Double s. Cannot target other allies, wards , or buildings .
  • Applies a cyclone effect on the target upon cast, so it dispels , turns it invulnerable and fully disables it.
  • While in the air, other units may pass below the cycloned unit.
  • The damage is applied at the end of the duration. If the cyclone gets purged, it still applies the damage as soon as the target lands.
  • The damage is only applied to enemy targets.
  • Can be cast on Roshan , but has no effect, since Roshan is immune to forced movement. The damage is still applied.


Recommended heroes [ edit ]

Crystal Maiden
  • Eul's Scepter improves Crystal Maiden's very slow movement speed, and gives her a temporary defense mechanism.
Death Prophet
  • Eul's Scepter increases Death Prophet's already fast movement speed, and can keep her alive while her spirits are out during Exorcism .
Invoker
  • Eul's Scepter is one of the best ways to set up and position abilities combos with Sun Strike , Ice Wall , EMP , Chaos Meteor , and Deafening Blast .
Jakiro
  • Eul's Scepter helps set up Ice Path , compensating for Jakiro's slow cast animations.
Lina
  • Eul's Scepter helps Lina land Light Strike Array .
Lion
  • Eul's Scepter gives Lion a bit more movement speed, a means of self defense, and an extra disable to go with Hex and Earth Spike .
Puck
  • In conjunction with Phase Shift , Eul's Scepter keeps Puck out of enemy reach, allowing more time for his numerous elusive abilities and items to come off cooldown.
Rubick
  • Eul's Scepter provides Rubick with his only means of escape, and helps him avoid initiations so he can safely steal abilities after Cyclone ends.
Shadow Fiend
  • Eul's Scepter is an excellent set up for Requiem of Souls , allowing Shadow Fiend to place himself directly on top of the target.
Skywrath Mage
  • Eul's Scepter helps Skywrath Mage survive a little longer, and can be a good set up for Mystic Flare .
Techies
  • Eul's Scepter provides much needed mana regeneration for Techies to lay more mines, and helps him set up mines under a Cycloned target.
  • Techies can also survive longer by Cycloning himself after using Blast Off! , while his team follows up.
Zeus
  • Eul's Scepter improves Zeus' low mobility, and keeps his mana up for more frequent nuking.

Others [ edit ]

  • Bloodseeker can use the Cyclone to set up Blood Rite , preventing the target from moving out of its radius. It's also widely used to prevent enemies from fleeing with Town Portal Scroll .
  • Timbersaw can place his Chakram underneath the Cyclone, thus hitting and slowing the target immediately after landing. He can also position himself for an easy Timber Chain . Since Timbersaw is reliant on his abilities for escape, using the Cyclone to dispel silences can ensure his survival if the enemy has no other follow-up disables.

Tips [ edit ]

  • Use the Cyclone offensively by disabling an enemy before a gank, giving you the time to set up your abilities.
  • Use the Cyclone on fleeing enemies to give your team the time to surround the target.
  • Use the Cyclone on enemies in teamfights to take them out of the fight for a few seconds. This is especially effective on carries.
  • Use the Cyclone on yourself during teamfights, baiting enemies into using their abilities on you, then becoming invulnerable to interrupt their burst damage.
  • Use the Cyclone to buy yourself a little more time for abilities and items to come off cooldown, especially items like Blink Dagger or Blade Mail .
  • Use the Cyclone on an enemy who has activated items like Blade Mail , Satanic , or Lotus Orb . Doing so will waste the duration of those items.
  • Eul's Scepter provides a cheap and beneficial dispel for support heroes who are very succebtible to silences. So when facing a lot of silences, or other strong but dispellable effects, Eul's Scepter is usually the best option.
  • The 40 bonus movement speed works well with other items that increase movement speed, such as Phase Boots .

Trivia [ edit ]

  • The scepter's name is a reference to Eul , the creator of the first DotA map back in 2003.
