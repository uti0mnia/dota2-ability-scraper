Shiva's Guard is an item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Armor . However, it can only be completed with items from the Secret Shop .

Additional information [ edit ]

  • One Shiva's Guard grants a total of 15 armor , 330 mana , 1.2 mana regeneration and 2.14% spell damage (and 30 attack damage to intelligence heroes).

  • Provides 800 radius flying vision , following the caster and leaving a trail of vision which lasts for 2 seconds.

Recommended heroes [ edit ]

Axe
  • Arctic Blast will hit multiple enemies when initiated on by Berserker's Call .
  • Arctic Blast slows fleeing enemies, and helps Axe bring them low enough for Culling Blade .
Batrider
  • Arctic Blast helps Batrider deal extra damage whenever he blinks in to grab an enemy with Flaming Lasso .
  • Arctic Blast allows Batrider to stay ahead of targets, burning them with the flaming trail from Firefly while slowing them further with Sticky Napalm .
Bristleback
  • Shiva's Guard's armor bonus stacks well with Bristleback's durability.
  • Arctic Blast's slow stacks with Viscous Nasal Goo , reducing enemies to a crawl.
Clockwerk
  • Arctic Blast boosts Clockwerk's otherwise low damage output.
  • Arctic Blast is useful for initiation, especially if used just before landing a Hookshot .
Dark Seer
  • Arctic Blast hit numerous heroes when caught together with Vacuum .
  • Arctic Blast's slow prevents enemies from fleeing illusions spawned by Wall of Replica , as well as allies hastened by Surge .
Death Prophet
  • Shiva's Guard's armor and attack speed slow keeps Death Prophet alive longer during Exorcism .
  • Arctic Blast's slow keeps enemies within range of Spirit Siphon and Exorcism .
Doom
  • Shiva's Guard makes up for Doom's low base armor.
  • Arctic Blast's slow keeps enemies within range of Scorched Earth and Aghanim's Scepter upgraded Doom .
Phoenix
  • Arctic Blast's slow stacks with Icarus Dive 's slow, making it hard for enemies to flee when Phoenix initiates on them.
  • Icarus Dive 's movement path allows Arctic Blast's radius to cover a very large area, and will hit almost all enemies on the screen.
  • Freezing Aura's attack speed slow makes it less likely for enemies to kill Phoenix during Supernova .
Storm Spirit
  • Ball Lightning 's rapid movement allows Storm Spirit to catch many enemies with Arctic Blast.
Tidehunter
  • Shiva's Guard's extra armor works well with Kraken Shell , making Tidehunter even tankier.
  • Shiva's Guard's extra intelligence gives Tidehunter a much larger mana pool, allowing to him comfortably use Ravage twice with a Refresher Orb .
  • Ravage ensures that most enemies will be hit by Arctic Blast.
Timbersaw
  • Timber Chain 's rapid movement helps Timbersaw hit more enemies with Arctic Blast.
  • Arctic Blast makes it easier to hit and keep enemies inside Chakram 's radius.
Underlord
  • Shiva's Guard's extra armor and attack speed slow makes Underlord relatively tanky, allowing him to teamfight longer with Atrophy Aura .

Others [ edit ]

  • Alchemist 's high regeneration through Chemical Rage makes up for the minor damage received after reduction from Shiva's Guard's armor, protecting him from being killed by burst damage.
  • Elder Titan benefits from Shiva's Guard's armor, allowing him to stay in the middle of fights and debuff enemies with Natural Order , as well as deal damage with Astral Spirit 's attack bonus.
  • Invoker can follow up with Arctic Blast after using Tornado or Deafening Blast , adding to his overall nuke damage.
  • Magnus is guaranteed to hit multiple enemies with Arctic Blast after landing a good Reverse Polarity , which prevents them from quickly spreading out after the stun ends.
  • Outworld Devourer benefits greatly from Shiva's Guard's extra intelligence, which lets him deal significantly more damage with Sanity's Eclipse .
  • Razor can use Arctic Blast to keep enemies within range of all his abilities.
  • Sand King can use Arctic Blast to keep enemies within range of his Epicenter .
  • Undying can use Arctic Blast to supplement his low damage, as well as keeping enemies within range of Tombstone 's zombies.

Tips [ edit ]

  • Arctic Blast 's effect hits every enemy once within its radius, even after its edge has expanded past the target. Because of its slow expansion time (taking about 2 seconds to reach max radius), it can be thought of as a moving area effect, centered on the hero.
    • Use Arctic Blast before blinking or engaging an enemy to instantly deal area damage upon initiation.
    • This can be used to instantly disable enemy Blink Dagger s by prematurely using Arctic Blast out of vision and then blinking onto an enemy.
  • Shiva's Guard is a useful item against enemies with mana burn . It greatly increases a hero's mana pool, and its aura slows the number of mana burning attacks made against its owner.
  • Shiva's Guard is good against heroes and and creeps with low attack speed, causing them to attack even slower.

Trivia [ edit ]

  • The name "Shiva" is a reference to the Shiva of the Final Fantasy series, an elemental summon with the power of ice.
  • Shiva is the supreme deity within Shaivism , one of the three most influential denominations in contemporary Hinduism.
