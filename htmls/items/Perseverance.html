Perseverance
L
A gem that grants heart to the bearer.
Cost
1700
Bought From
Common
Bonus +6 Health regeneration
+125% Mana regeneration
+10 Attack damage
Disassemble? Yes
Alert allies? No
Recipe

Perseverance is an item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Common. It can also be completed solely with items from the Side Lane Shop.

Additional information[edit | edit source]

  • Perseverance is partially shareable.
