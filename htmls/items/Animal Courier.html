Additional information[edit | edit source]

  • Animal Courier is completely shareable.
  • Animal Courier has a 2 minutes stock cooldown.
  • Animal Courier can be upgraded to a Flying Courier.

Ability[edit | edit source]

Deploy Courier
Play
Ability
No Target
Deploys a creature to carry items to and from your team's base.
0

Notes:

  • Interrupts the user's channeling spells upon cast.
  • The Animal Courier is not a valid target for most spells. A list of spells which affect the courier can be found below.
  • It is possible to have multiple Animal Couriers. However, only the first created courier reacts on the courier button.
  • All couriers are controllable by the entire team, no matter who buys and deploys the courier.
  • When a courier dies, the opposing team's heroes get 150 gold each. If there already is a dead courier, no gold is granted.
    • However, an enemy making the killing blow on a courier always gets the 150 gold bounty.
  • The Animal Courier is invulnerable when near its team's fountain.
  • When an Animal Courier dies, it respawns in 120 seconds at the team's fountain. While dead, items in its inventory are unaccessable.


Courier inventory[edit | edit source]

Like a hero, the courier possesses an inventory with 6 slots. However, it has restrictions. Almost every item gets muted when in the courier's inventory, rendering all their stats and abilities disabled and unusable for the courier. Muted items are darkened out in its inventory. The following items are not muted:

  • Animal Courier
  • Bottle
  • Cheese
  • Dust of Appearance
  • Gem of True Sight
  • Observer and Sentry Wards
  • Perseverance
  • Ring of Health
  • Ring of Regen
  • Sage's Mask
  • Smoke of Deceit
  • Tango (Shared)
  • Void Stone

However, only a few of them that can be actually used by the courier.

  • The Animal Courier has no mana pool and mana regeneration. So Sage's Mask, Void Stone and the mana regen part of Perseverance are useless for it. This also means that it cannot use Dust of Appearance and Town Portal Scroll.
  • The Animal Courier is not able to place or share Observer Wards and Sentry Wards. Trying to do so will give the "Item is not usable by Couriers" error message.
  • The Bottle can be "used". Using it puts it on its short cooldown, but does not use up a charge and does not regenerate. This goes for using it on self or on allies with the CTRL key.
  • Along with that, the Animal Courier can neither pick up runes, nor put them in the Bottle.
  • Despite not being muted, Gem of True Sight does not provide True Sight when carried by the Animal Courier.
  • When using Animal Courier from the courier's inventory, the new spawned courier will use the default donkey courier model.
  • It is possible to buy a Divine Rapier with the Animal Courier and it can drop the Rapier. However, it cannot pick it back up again, as it cannot pick up Rapiers in general. It also cannot pick up the Aegis of the Immortal.
  • Both Gem of True Sight and Divine Rapier are dropped when an Animal Courier carrying them is killed.
  • The courier can use Tango (Shared) and Cheese and benefits from the health regeneration granted by Ring of Regen, Ring of Health and Perseverance. It also can activate Smoke of Deceit, but it does not affect the courier.
