The Stout Shield is an item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Armaments , and at the Side Lane Shop .

Ability [ edit ]

Damage Block
Ability
Passive
Affects
Self
Gives a chance to block damage based on whether the equipped hero is melee or ranged.
Proc Chance : 50%
Blocked Damage (Melee) : 16
Blocked Damage (Ranged) : 8

Notes:

  • Does not work against wards ( Plague Ward , Mass Serpent Ward , Death Ward ).
  • Does not reduce physical damage from spells, except from Moon Glaive and The Swarm .
  • Damage Block uses pseudo-random distribution .
  • For more details, see Damage Block .


Recommended heroes [ edit ]

Axe
  • Axe's high natural health regeneration combined with Stout Shield makes him much tougher in lane, especially after picking up another health regeneration item.
Spirit Breaker
  • Stout Shield's damage block keeps Spirit Breaker alive when charging into enemy lines, where he often takes damage from multiple sources.
Timbersaw
  • Reactive Armor still triggers after Stout Shield's damage block, making Timbersaw extremely resilient in lane.
Ursa
  • Ursa generally needs a Stout Shield to jungle effectively.

Others [ edit ]

  • Melee carries who need extra protection from harassment in lane can benefit from a Stout Shield.
    • Chaos Knight
    • Riki
    • Sven
    • Juggernaut
    • Slark
    • Anti-Mage
    • Monkey King
    • Lifestealer
    • Troll Warlord
    • Bloodseeker
    • Dragon Knight
    • Alchemist

Tips [ edit ]

  • Useful for tanking creep attacks as a melee hero, and more effective than a Ring of Protection against enemy physical attacks in the early game.
