The Guardian Greaves are an item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Support . However, it requires an item from the Secret Shop or the Side Lane Shop to be completed.

Additional information [ edit ]

  • Movement speed bonuses from multiple pairs of boots do not stack.
  • Stacks with all other movement speed bonuses.
  • One pair of Guardian Greaves grants a total of 100 health , 0.3 health regeneration , 5.71 armor , 5 attack speed , 305 mana , 0.2 mana regeneration , 0.36% spell damage and 5 attack damage .
    • Together with the aura, it grants a total of (11 while boosted) health regeneration and 7.71 (20.71 while boosted) armor to the owner.

  • The aura 's buff lingers for 0.5 seconds.


Recommended heroes [ edit ]

Omniknight
  • Omniknight's high armor stacks well with Guardian Greaves' armor bonus.
  • Purification can be used with Guardian Greaves for burst healing.
Necrophos
  • Necrophos is not dependent on items to deal damage, and Death Pulse works well with Guardian Greaves to heal his team in bursts.
Underlord
  • Underlord's high health means he can be in the center of fights, where Guardian Greaves' aura and heal reach the most heroes.
Undying
  • Combined with the durability from Flesh Golem , Guardian Greaves' regeneration and armor makes Undying's team much more resilient.
Jakiro
  • Jakiro is not dependent on items to be effective, and therefore picks up Guardian Greaves makes up for his vulnerability.
Dark Seer
  • Dark Seer does not need offensive items, and his very high base armor stacks well with Guardian Greaves.
  • Dark Seer's speed through Surge allows him to cover and use Guardian Greaves on his allies more easily.
Dazzle
  • Guardian Greaves' armor aura works well with the extra armor from Weave .
  • Shallow Grave gives Dazzle the time to greatly restore a dying ally's health with Guardian Greaves.
Bounty Hunter
  • A support Bounty Hunter benefits from Guardian Greaves' extra armor and health regeneration, and gives Bounty Hunter another function in teamfights besides debuffing enemies with Track .
Tidehunter
  • After getting his Blink Dagger , Tidehunter's toughness through Kraken Shell stacks well with Guardian Greaves' bonuses.
  • With a Refresher Orb , Tidehunter can use Guardian Greaves twice, giving him the mana to use Ravage twice as well.
Io
  • Guardian Greaves' restoration is doubled through Tether , although Io may not always be able to afford it.

Tips [ edit ]

  • Meepo clones with Aghanim's Scepter upgrade get double stats and armor from the item, which can offer high survivability against physical damage.
  • Tinker 's Rearm can refresh Greaves as well, offering high mana return for Tinker himself and the heal for heroes that are not having the Mend buff.
  • Because the boosted armor is only granted when the HP are under 20 percent an Heart of Tarrasque is an excellent pick combined with it.
