The Pipe of Insight is an Item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Support .

Additional information [ edit ]

  • The magic resistance stacks multiplicatively with other sources of magic resistance.
  • Increases most heroes' total magic resistance to 47.5%, assuming basic 25% magic resistance and no other sources of magic resistance.
    • Together with the aura, this is further increased to 52.75%.
  • Increases Meepo 's and Visage 's total magic resistance to 54.5% and 37% respectively, assuming their basic magic resistance and no other sources of magic resistance.
    • Together with the aura, this is further increased to 59.05% and 43.3%.
  • Pipe of Insight does not inherit the attribute bonuses from Headdress .
  • Together with the aura , one Pipe of Insight grants a total of 12 health regeneration to the owner.

Modifiers
Item Pipe Barrier: Undispellable.

Notes:

  • Interrupts the user's channeling spells upon cast.
  • Blocks damage before any reductions , except when spell immune , during which it does not block any magical damage.
  • Recasting Barrier refreshes the buff and resets the barrier's absorb capacity.
  • Does not stack with other magical damage barriers .
    • If multiple sources of barriers are active on a unit, all of them lose capacity from the same absorbed damage instance.
  • Only blocks magical damage, not spell damage of other types.
  • Does not share cooldown with Hood of Defiance .


Insight Aura
Ability
Aura
Affects
Allies
Gives allied units 4 health regeneration and 10% magic resistance in a 900 radius.
Radius : 900
Health Regeneration Bonus : 4
Magic Resistance Bonus : 10%
Illusions provide the aura, but do not benefit from it.
Modifiers
Item Pipe Aura: Undispellable.

Notes:

  • Stacks with all other sources of flat health regeneration and magic resistance , except with Insight Aura from multiple Pipes of Insight.
  • Increases most heroes' total magic resistance to 32.5%, assuming basic 25% magic resistance and no other sources of magic resistance.
  • Increases Meepo 's and Visage 's total magic resistance to 41.5% and 19% respectively, assuming their basic magic resistance and no other sources of magic resistance.
  • Does not affect wards , buildings and invulnerable units.
  • The aura 's buff lingers for 0.5 seconds.


Recommended heroes [ edit ]

  • Tough tank heroes with high health regeneration benefit even more from Pipe of Insight's regeneration.
    • Timbersaw
    • Axe
    • Bristleback
    • Pudge
    • Tidehunter
  • Tough support heroes are ideal for carrying Pipe of Insight.
    • Dark Seer
    • Underlord
    • Abaddon
    • Phoenix
    • Undying
    • Elder Titan

Trivia [ edit ]

  • In the transition from DotA to Dota 2, Pipe of Insight name was changed from the Khadgar's Pipe of Insight to simply Pipe of Insight. Khadgar was a wizard in Warcraft II.
  • Dazzle 's cosmetic item Pipe of Dezun is based on the Pipe of Insight.
