The Quelling Blade is an item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Armaments , and at the Side Lane Shop .

Abilities [ edit ]

Chop Tree or Ward
Ability
Target Unit
Affects
Trees / Enemy Units
Destroy a target tree or ward. Chop cast range is increased when targeting wards.
Cast Range (Tree) : 350
Cast Range (Ward) : 450
4

Notes:

  • Can only target trees (including Sprout trees) and enemy Observer Ward s and Sentry Ward s.
  • Can also target an enemy Techies ' Stasis Trap s and Remote Mines , using the same cast range as for wards.
  • Does not share cooldown with Battle Fury or Iron Talon .


Quell
Ability
Passive
Affects
Enemy Units
Increases attack damage against non-hero units by 24 for melee heroes, and 7 for ranged.
Attack Damage Bonus (Melee) : 24
Attack Damage Bonus (Ranged) : 7

Notes:

  • Stacks with the Quell of Battle Fury , but not with Iron Talon and additional Quelling Blades.
  • The bonus damage does not apply when attacking friendly units (i.e. denying own creeps).
  • When the owner's attack type changes to ranged or melee, the attack damage bonus adapts immediately.
  • Kunkka doesn't benefit from quell when he doesn't have Tidebringer learned.
  • Quell fully works with crits , cleaves and lifesteal . When having cleave , attacking a creep cleaves the increased damage in the area.
    • However, if the unit has Tidebringer , the attack which procs it is not increased by Quell.
    • When Tidebringer is on cooldown, the attack will be increased by Quell.
  • Elder Dragon Form 's splash also splashes the increased damage when attacking a creep.
  • The damage Moon Glaive bounces are not increased, since they are based on the attacker's attack damage value.
  • Psi Blades will spread the increased damage when attacking a creep.
  • Does not work against wards , buildings , Spirit Bear and allied units.


Recommended heroes [ edit ]

  • Quelling Blade is usually purchased by melee carries who need to ensure that they can get last hits in lane.

Tips [ edit ]

  • The 24 bonus damage to creep on melee heroes is good for last hitting and jungling.
    • You can lifesteal from the bonus damage.
